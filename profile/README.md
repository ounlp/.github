### 🔬 What we study

We work toward **reliable human–AI language systems** — from multi-party dialogue and LLM
agents to structured prediction and trustworthy deployment in education, healthcare, and science.

| Theme | |
|---|---|
| **🗣️ Dialogue & Discourse** | Multi-party, multi-modal dialogue systems and discourse-aware modeling. |
| **🧩 Structured Prediction** | Discourse-aware representation learning and structured prediction over language. |
| **🤖 LLM Alignment & Agents** | LLM alignment and agentic systems that act and collaborate. |
| **🎛️ Multimodal Interaction** | Grounded communication across speech, vision, and text. |
| **🔭 AI for Science** | ML for discovery across materials, chemistry, and the physical sciences. |
| **🛡️ Trustworthy AI** | Reliable, interpretable AI for education and healthcare. |
